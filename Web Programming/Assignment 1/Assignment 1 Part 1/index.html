ABOUT US

ALOW provides on a non-profit basis accommodation in which charitable or non-profit organizations and associations, which meet the admission criteria of the Corporation, render services to the needy and the poor of the Regional Municipality of Ottawa-Carleton and the surrounding areas. ALOW empowers people and communities to live with renewed dignity and hope. Through our 45,000 square feet of affordable offices and facilities, non-profits provide direct services to diverse populations. You will also find an active cultural environment, with an 850 seat performance theatre, 200 seat community hall, and rehearsal rooms. We foster a positive environment where tenants, patrons, and community can be productive and successful. We are a registered charity whose revenue comes primarily from facilities rentals and occasional one time grants.
